  • I wouldn't say I'm in love with PHP - quite the opposite. It's just that it seems to have won the war so far in hosting penetration. I've had dalliances with Twisted over the past few years - and though I do dig quite a lot about it, it seems to have quite a high bar for adoption. (Keep in mind I've been considering WordPress as a role model.)

  • The more I think about the Zend Framework, the less I think I'm actually all that enamored with it. The docs need work, some bits seem missing or clunky, and I catch small hints here and there that there's a bit of flutter in the consistency of the framework's development process and goals. One funny thing, though, is that there's a Zend office almost within walking distance of our apartment. I wonder if I could walk down there and have a chat about things? :)

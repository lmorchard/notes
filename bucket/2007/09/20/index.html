  • Hello world. I missed you.

  • I have a Zeos Pocket PC. A serial port, a parallel port, and two PC card slots. No USB. DOS. Runs on 2 AA batteries, reads great in sunlight, and has a usable QWERTY keyboard. I used it in college to write quite a few papers and stories. I wonder if I could somehow get it working again usefully with a MacBook Pro and other modern technology for writing on the bus?

  • Finally checked out the new Apple iPods in person this week. Love the iPod Touch. But, I'm not a big fan of the weird pillowy-bevel metal thing on the iPod Classic, nor that plus the squat-form of the new iPod Nano. I think I'd like the design if it were still some form of plastic and not aluminum and steel. Half-heartedly got an eye out to upgrade my 4th Gen 20GB iPod, but it won't be from this generation I don't think.

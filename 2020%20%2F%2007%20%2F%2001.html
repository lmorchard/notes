    • Makes me think of a process that's been doing on as long as I've been alive.
    • There were heirloom-quality tools my father & grandfathers bought from companies that have essentially ceased to exist. But the company names carry on, slapped onto inferior products in the hopes that I'll buy them.
    • Maybe falling for a golden age myth, but...
    • Imagine a company or a group that makes good products or does good work.
    • That group gets taken over or purchased.
    • The new management carries on with the same name and external messaging as always
    • Meanwhile, the innards are hollowed out, all corners cut, all processes by which anything good came about in the first place are demolished
    • The positive momentum built by the real deal before the co-opting fuels a harvest for the new management
    • The thing collapses, the new management moves on
    • Feels like that process is coming close to completion for my entire country

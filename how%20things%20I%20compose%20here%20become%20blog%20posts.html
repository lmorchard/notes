how things I compose here become blog posts

I have a notion that I'd like to produce more blog posts. I seem to remember a golden age when I blogged more.

I'm collecting thoughts here, intending to do so loosely and not necessarily coherently.

I'd assert that a blog post should be more coherent and polished.

Maybe that's worthy? Maybe I shouldn't worry about it so much?

Blog posts are singular publications.

Notes here are evolving intersections because my brain is a pen plotter drawing unoptimized paths.

Can I find reliably identifiable points where a blog post can be extracted as a critical mass snapshot of notes?
